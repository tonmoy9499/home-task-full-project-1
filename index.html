THE LION ZOO


The lion is a wild animal. The jungle is its natural habitat. It is called “the King of the Jungle” because of its massive size and raw power. It has a strong build, four legs, a large head with mane (in males), a tail and two glowing eyes which make it look ferocious. The lion is famous for its roar and hunting ability. It lives in a pride.
As well as being known as the king of the jungle, the lion is also the world's most dangerous animal. He is the best hunter in the forest. His biological name is Panthera leo, as he is very large and powerful.

AWESOME RARE LIONS

White lions are not albinos. Their white color is caused by a recessive trait, called leucism, derived from a less-severe mutation in the same gene that causes albinism, distinct from the gene responsible for white tigers.
